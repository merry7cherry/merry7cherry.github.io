Chenrui Ma

Ph.D. Student @ UVA · MSCS @ UCI

I will be joining the University of Virginia as a Ph.D. student in Computer Science, where I will be affiliated with the RAISE Lab and advised by Prof. Ferdinando Fioretto. Currently, I am a Master's student at the University of California, Irvine, working as a Research Assistant in the Trustworthy ML/AI Group advised by Prof. Yanning Shen. I am also a student collaborator at Oak Ridge National Laboratory.

I received my bachelor's degree from Central South University (中南大学). During my senior year, I studied as an exchange student at the University of California, Irvine, where I completed my undergraduate thesis and graduate-level coursework. My Chinese name is 马晨睿.

I am seeking a summer internship focused on generative and multimodal understanding models for real-world data, including images and videos. I am motivated to tackle challenges in generation, perception, reasoning, and efficiency.
